Youfa Liureceived the M.S. degree in Mathematics from the Wuhan Institute of Physics and Mathematics, Chinese Academy of Sciences, Beijing, China, in 2017, and Ph.D. degree in computer science from Wuhan University, Wuhan, China, in 2020. Now he acts as an Associate Professor in School of Computer Science, Wuhan University, China. He has published some papers in top journals, such as IJCV, IEEE TIP/TNNLS/TMM, ACM TOMM/TKDD, Information Sciences, Neural Networks and Pattern Recognition. His research interests include machine learning and computer vision.
